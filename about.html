About Lisa

Born in Changsha, Hunan Province, China. She draws with her unique sensibility and is especially skilled in creating paintings of flowers and birds. She has won many gold medals in Chinese calligraphy and painting contests, and is highly acclaimed. Her works are included in "Dictionary of Famous Chinese Calligraphers".
